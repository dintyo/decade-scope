Not Included

Clear boundaries

Customer Account Migration

Customer accounts and passwords cannot be transferred between platforms. Existing customers will need to create new accounts on the Shopify store. This is standard for all platform migrations.

Order History Migration

Historical orders will not be migrated to Shopify.

Recommendation

Keep your BigCommerce hosting active for 6 months after launch. Your staff can reference historical orders in the system they already know, rather than learning to navigate imported data in a new format. This approach works better in practice.

Also Not Included

  • Third-party app subscriptions (reviews, email marketing, etc.)
  • Payment gateway setup (you configure Shopify Payments)
  • Custom app development
  • Ongoing maintenance beyond revision hours
  • Content creation or copywriting
